Courses
Diploma in Yoga with Wellness Applications

This course focuses on the principles and practices of yoga combined with modern wellness techniques. It helps students develop physical fitness, mental clarity, and emotional balance. The program includes yoga postures, meditation, breathing techniques, and holistic health practices for overall well-being.

Diploma in Naturopathy Therapist

This course provides in-depth knowledge of natural healing methods. It trains students in therapies such as diet therapy, hydrotherapy, mud therapy, and lifestyle management. The goal is to promote healing without medicines by using nature-based treatments.

Diploma in Naturopathy & Nutrition

This program combines naturopathy with scientific nutrition to promote a healthy lifestyle. Students learn about balanced diets, natural remedies, detoxification, and preventive healthcare. It prepares individuals to guide others in maintaining long-term health and wellness.
